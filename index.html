Slow Device Support

Is your PC or laptop running at a crawl? I diagnose and fix the root cause — whether it's software bloat, overheating, failing drives, or just needing a tune-up — and get your device running fast again.

Virus & Malware Removal

Suspicious behaviour, pop-ups, or sluggish performance after clicking something you shouldn't have? I'll fully clean your system, remove threats, and harden it against future attacks.

RAM upgrades, SSD replacements, new GPU or CPU installs, fan replacements — if it's inside your machine, I can swap it out or upgrade it to give your system a new lease of life.
